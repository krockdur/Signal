30s

Updated : 18/04/2023

30s is a vertical shooter. you have 30 seconds of life.

Status : In progress for the GamedevJs 2023 jam.

How to play :

Left click => Fire

Right click => bombs

Combo :

red - white - red give you a bomb

Yellow | Green | Yellow | White | Red will fill your life time
